E-8T

E-8T is a food recommendation bot that helps you find great eats nearby!

At the moment, E-8T is only available in Singapore and will make recommendations from HungryGoWhere when you submit your location. You can also do a general search by type of food, cuisine or restaurant.
Stay tuned for exciting enhancements such as personalized recommendations, listings from more food review websites and proactive lunch suggestions!
